# Sherwin-Williams Paints, Stains, Supplies and Coating Solutions

> Sherwin-Williams’ main public gateway site for paint colors, painting tools,
> DIY inspiration, professional products/resources, industrial coatings portals,
> and corporate/investor/careers information. Primary audiences include
> homeowners/DIYers, painting contractors & pros, designers/architects, and
> industrial customers. The page is navigation-heavy (mega menu + carousels) and
> links out to multiple Sherwin-Williams sub-sites (industrial, corporate,
> investors, careers, privacy/accessibility, suppliers) and major social
> channels.

This page is a global “hub”/gateway with:

 - A **region selector modal** (country/region selection; content varies by
   locale).
 - A **utility bar** (Find a Store, Select Region, Sign In).
 - A large **mega-navigation** grouping content into: “Shop Products & Color”,
   “Services & Resources”, “Industrial Coatings”, and “Our Company”.
 - Multiple **content carousels** (marketing hero carousel, top colors carousel,
   performance coatings carousel, news cards).
 - Structured data present: **schema.org Organization** (includes brand
   relationships and social profiles).

Structured elements & how to interpret them:

 - **Primary tasks**: explore colors, order samples, use color tools, locate
   stores, sign in to PRO+.
 - **Color content hierarchy** is organized by families, collections, rooms,
   exterior areas, and tools.
 - **Professional content** includes product categories, specifications, data
   sheets, calculators, PRO+ program, and business tools.
 - **Industrial coatings** content is largely hosted on
   `industrial.sherwin-williams.com` and subdivided by industry vertical.
 - **Corporate/company** content is hosted on `corporate.sherwin-williams.com`,
   `investors.sherwin-williams.com`, and `careers.sherwin-williams.com`.

Outbound links below include **all external domains referenced** plus key
first-party deep links shown in navigation/carousels.
